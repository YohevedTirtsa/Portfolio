Android App

In 2009, two pharmaceutical giants Galaxy and Swiss Bourdin joined forces to create a leader in this industrial sector. Once the laboratory has been created, the Galaxy Swiss Bourdin entity wishes to standardize the management of professional expenses for medical representatives through the internal development of an Android application. The GSB laboratory now wishes to dedicate an Android application for medical visitors who are often in the field, and who therefore need a mobile solution. It will allow you to declare fixed and non-fixed costs, to consult the costs in their entirety and to send the persisted data to the central database.
  • Date : 2020
  • Skills : Java, SQL
  • Softwares : Android Studio, SQlite, Sdk, AVD Manager, GITHUB
