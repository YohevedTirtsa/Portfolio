Web Application

In 2009, two pharmaceutical giants Galaxy and Swiss Bourdin joined forces to create a leader in this industrial sector. Once the laboratory has been created, the Galaxy Swiss Bourdin entity wishes to standardize the management of professional expenses for medical representatives through the in-house development of a web application called Gsb_AppliFrais. This application is intended for both medical visitors and accountants in charge of their management. The part intended for medical representatives is developed. It is now a matter of developing the part intended for accountants in charge of validating and paying these professional fees.
  • Date : 2020
  • Skills : PHP, MySQL, HTML,CSS
  • Softwares : NetBeans, PhpMyAdmin, Xdebug, PHPdocumentor, WampServer
